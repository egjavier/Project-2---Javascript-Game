Memory Game

    How the Game Work:

  • Match pairs of cards while preserving your 6 lives.
  • The player begins with 6 lives.
    The game ends when you run out of lives or successfully match all the pairs.
  • The player flips over two cards, revealing their faces.

    • The game continues until one of the following conditions is met:
  • The player successfully matches all the pairs, in which case they win the game.
    The player runs out of lives, resulting in a loss.


    • Hit PLAY to start!
